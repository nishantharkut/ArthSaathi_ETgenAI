# ArthSaathi — Impact Model

*Condensed from `EXECUTION_PLAN.md` Part 6 — submission-ready.*

## The problem (quantified)

- **27.06 crore** mutual fund folios in India (AMFI, Feb 2026)
- **₹82.03 lakh crore** total industry AUM (AMFI, Feb 2026)
- **₹21,107 crore/year** in distributor commissions on regular plans (AMFI FY 2024–25)
- **~51%** of AUM still in regular plans (Cafemutual, Dec 2025)
- **5.9 crore** unique investors (PANs) — many pay unnecessary regular-plan costs
- **Zero** widely available free AI tools that combine CAS parsing, multi-agent analysis, and mentor-style guidance

## Per-user impact (illustrative)

For a typical portfolio (~₹32.5L, mixed regular/direct), switching regular plans to direct and reducing overlap can save **tens of thousands of rupees per year** in TER drag — compounding to **lakhs over a decade** (see live **Wealth Gap** and **expense** sections in the app).

## How ArthSaathi creates impact

1. **Awareness** — XIRR, overlap, TER, and benchmark alpha in one flow.
2. **Actionable output** — Rebalancing narrative + goal planner + tax hints + mentor chat grounded in *their* data.
3. **Accessibility** — Analysis from a standard CAS PDF; **Try sample data** for judges without a PDF.

## Social impact

Financial literacy, transparency on fees, and inclusion for anyone who can export a CAS — no paid advisor gate.
